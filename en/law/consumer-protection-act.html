Consumer Protection Law

Royal Decree No. 66/2014 issuing

Consumer Protection Law

We Qaboos bin Said, Sultan of Oman

After reviewing the Basic Law of the State issued by Royal Decree No. 101/96,

And the Consumer Protection Law promulgated by Royal Decree No. 81/2002,

After presentation to the Oman Council,

Based on the public interest.

We drew what is coming

● Article one

The provisions of the attached Consumer Protection Law shall apply.

● second subject

This decree shall be published in the Official Gazette.

Issued on: 7 Safar 1436 AH

Corresponding to: November 30, 2014 AD
